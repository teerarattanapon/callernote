Privacy Policy for CallerNote

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CallerNote. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our core services, we may collect the following information:

  • Call Log & Phone State: We require access to your call logs and phone state to detect incoming/outgoing calls and display your custom notes.
  • User-Generated Content: Any notes or text you input and associate with specific phone numbers.
  • Contact Information: Access to your contacts to match names with phone numbers for your notes.
  • Device Information: Basic technical data such as device model and OS version to improve app performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To display your saved notes during active phone calls.
  • To allow you to manage and organize notes for your contacts.
  • To improve the functionality and user experience of the app.

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: By default, your notes are stored locally on your device.

Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your call data or notes with any external advertising agencies or data brokers.

6. Permissions

The app will request the following permissions:

  • READ_CALL_LOG / READ_PHONE_STATE: To identify the caller.
  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: To display the "Note" overlay on top of the calling screen.
  • READ_CONTACTS: To sync notes with your contact list.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, edit, or delete your notes at any time.
  • Revoke app permissions through your device settings.
  • Uninstall the app, which will remove locally stored data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
